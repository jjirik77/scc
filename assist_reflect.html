Where do you already see AI assistants?

Think about your daily life as a student, worker, or consumer. Where do you already see AI assistants or chatbot-like tools? In 3–4 sentences, name one example, what it does well, and one thing you wish it did differently.

Your response is for your own learning; if you'd like to keep it, copy and save it before moving on.

Example response "A bank's website chat helped me find my routing number at midnight—fast and available when no branch was open. One thing I'd change: it should hand me off to a real person sooner once my question gets complicated."
Need ideas?

You've probably used more of these than you realize. Consider:

  • Banking chat — checking a balance or finding account info on a bank's site or app
  • Shopping support bots — order tracking, returns, or product questions on retail sites
  • School help tools — a college website chatbot, LMS assistant, or tutoring helper
  • Customer service chat — airlines, phone carriers, streaming services, delivery apps
  • Phone assistants — Siri, Google Assistant, or Alexa setting timers, answering questions, or sending texts
